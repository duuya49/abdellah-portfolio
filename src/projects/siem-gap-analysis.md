<h3 class="tech-section-title">What I did</h3>
<p>Evaluated log coverage across enterprise systems to ensure critical telemetry was correctly ingested and parsed for security monitoring.</p>

<h3 class="tech-section-title">Execution Steps</h3>
<ul class="step-list">
    <li>Identified critical business assets and their corresponding log sources across the enterprise.</li>
    <li>Verified log ingestion status and parsing accuracy within the central SIEM platform.</li>
    <li>Detected critical blind spots and missing telemetry from high-priority assets.</li>
    <li>Collaborated with IT and DevOps teams to onboard and tune missing log sources.</li>
    <li>Validated visibility by testing specific detection use cases against newly onboarded logs.</li>
</ul>
