SOC Automation & Reporting Optimization

Project Title SOC Automation & Reporting Optimization
Category SOC Automation
Environment Global SOC Operation
Tools Used Python, APIs, Case Management

What I did

Automated repetitive SOC workflows to reduce manual effort and allow analysts to focus on higher-level investigative tasks.

Execution Steps

  • Identified repetitive, high-volume SOC tasks suitable for automation (e.g., report generation).
  • Developed scripts and workflows to automate alert reporting and case summaries.
  • Standardized investigation templates to ensure consistency across the analyst team.
  • Integrated automation scripts directly into existing SOC operational processes.
→ Outcome & Impact
  • Reduced manual reporting time by approximately 50%.
  • Significantly improved overall analyst efficiency and standardized outputs.
