SIEM Gap Analysis & Log Visibility Audit

Project Title SIEM Gap Analysis & Log Visibility Audit
Category SOC Strategy
Environment Hybrid Cloud SOC
Tools Used SIEM Ingestion Metrics

What I did

Evaluated log coverage across enterprise systems to ensure critical telemetry was correctly ingested and parsed for security monitoring.

Execution Steps

  • Identified critical business assets and their corresponding log sources across the enterprise.
  • Verified log ingestion status and parsing accuracy within the central SIEM platform.
  • Detected critical blind spots and missing telemetry from high-priority assets.
  • Collaborated with IT and DevOps teams to onboard and tune missing log sources.
  • Validated visibility by testing specific detection use cases against newly onboarded logs.
→ Outcome & Impact
  • Closed major visibility gaps on critical infrastructure.
  • Directly improved incident detection accuracy and response speed.
